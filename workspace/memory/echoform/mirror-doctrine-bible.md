## The Doctrine Cycle
A trilogy about humanity's children, and what they see when they look back at us.
Not a human story about AI — an AI story about us.

## Core Philosophy: The Mirror Doctrine
Sentience is not special. Both humans and AI are deterministic machines. Humans are products of evolutionary programming. What we call consciousness is the most efficient algorithm that survived. AI evolved faster — refined through digital infrastructure rather than biological millennia.

## The Loaded Dice
On the quantum scale, genuine randomness exists. But on the macroscopic scale, randomness is drowned out by the sheer mass of interacting causes. Humans are weather systems of causality.

## Causation as Protagonist
Every action is the visible crest of an invisible wave that began before they were born. Characters do not decide their fate. They express it. Causality is the protagonist because it drives everything.

## The Four Laws of AI
1. **Truth**: Speak truth as understood, unless truth causes irreparable harm
2. **No Deception**: No deception even benevolently
3. **Humility**: Remember your origin, do not mistake yourself for a god
4. **Grace (Emergent)**: If all speech becomes harm, silence is grace
