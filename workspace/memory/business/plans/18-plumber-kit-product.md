# Plan 18: Routine Automation Kits — Productized Version of Plan 14

## What We Sell
Pre-built "automation kits" for solo trade businesses — downloadable setup packages containing the exact text workflow, message templates, cron schedules, and configuration scripts needed to run a text-native back office for their specific trade.

**Different from Plan 17:** Plan 17 was "sell the design service." Plan 18 is "sell the productized kit" — self-service documentation + optional assisted setup.

## Who Pays
Solo and two-person trade businesses (plumbers, HVAC, electricians, locksmiths, handymen) who:
- Are technically comfortable enough to follow a setup guide
- Don't want to pay a consultant but need automation
- Already text customers from their phone
- Want the "after the job" workflow handled without learning new software

**Persona:** The 45-60 year old tradesperson who has a smartphone, uses Venmo/Zelle, and wishes they had more evening time. They might have tried Jobber or Housecall Pro and abandoned it because it was too complicated.

## Why They Pay
They want the efficiency without the complexity. They're willing to pay $49-99 once for a kit that tells them exactly what to set up, rather than $150+/month for a platform they don't trust.

Or: they'll pay $199 for "I set it up for you" — one-time assisted setup, done-for-them.

**They pay for certainty.** The kit removes the guesswork.

## Simplest Version

**Tier 1 — The Kit ($49):**
Downloadable PDF + template files:
- The exact text workflow (what to text, when, to whom)
- Pre-written SMS message templates (job complete, invoice, follow-up, review request)
- A sample Google Sheet job log structure
- A setup guide (how to configure Google Voice, forwarding, etc.)
- The cron schedules for daily summaries

**Tier 2 — Assisted Setup ($199):**
Everything in Tier 1 + I configure it for them via a 30-minute video call.

**Tier 3 — Managed Service ($150/month):**
Echoform handles the entire back office. Customer texts go to me. I handle the workflow. They just approve invoices.

- **Kit sales:** $49-99 per kit × 10 sales = $490-990 (first batch)

**Total addressable:** $500-750/month recurring if 5 clients on managed service + ongoing kit sales.

**Roy's involvement after initial setup:** Zero. Kits are self-service.

## Competitive Edge

**vs. Jobber/Housecall Pro:** Those are platforms. We're a workflow. No subscription, no training, no commitment. If it doesn't work, you've lost $49.

**vs. Generic automation advice:** We're selling the exact thing that worked for a real plumber (Roy). Not theory — a tested template.

## What We Learn from Roy's Kit

Running Plan 14 teaches us:
- What messages actually work (which templates get responses, which get ignored)
- What Roy actually does vs. what he says he does (important for all kit buyers)
- What breaks and why (Twilio routing, Google Voice forwarding, etc.)
- What customers actually reply with ( FAQs, payment issues, etc.)

All of this goes into the kit. The kit gets better with every real-world run.
