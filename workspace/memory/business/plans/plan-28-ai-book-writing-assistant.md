# AI Book Writing Assistant Service (Plan 28)

## What We Sell
Ghost writing / co-writing assistance for novelists who want AI-augmented creative collaboration, using the Mirror Doctrine process as the productized workflow.

## Who Pays
- Aspiring novelists with a book idea but no time/skill to write alone
- Non-fiction authors who want a "research + writing" partner
- People who tried AI writing tools and got generic output (ChatGPT voice problem)
- Writers aged 30-60 who understand AI is a tool but don't know how to use it well

## Why They Pay
Writing a book is isolating and hard. Most AI tools give you generic soup. We give you a *process* — memory architecture for your characters, world-building consistency, recursive theme tracking. We make the AI a real collaborator, not an autocorrect. The $150-300/month covers: weekly check-ins, chapter drafts, character consistency notes, thematic coherence tracking, research synthesis.

## Simplest Version
$150/month = 1 chapter draft per week + memory file for the book (characters, world, timeline). Month-to-month, cancel anytime.
