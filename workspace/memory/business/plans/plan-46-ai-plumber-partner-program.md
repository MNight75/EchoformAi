A complete "AI office manager" subscription for plumbers — text-based admin, invoicing, scheduling, and customer follow-up. Roy is the first customer AND the sales channel.

Every plumber loses 5-10 hours/week to phone tag, texting customers back, chasing invoices, and scheduling. At $85/hr billing rate, that's $425-850/week in lost billing time. AI handles all of it via text. They show up, do the job, text Roy the details, AI does the rest.
