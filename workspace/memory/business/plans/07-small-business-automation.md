# Plan 07: Small Business Automation

## What We Sell
We automate the administrative overhead of running a small business — scheduling, invoicing, follow-up emails, appointment reminders, and basic CRM — so owners can focus on billable work.

## Who Pays
Sole proprietors and small service businesses (plumbers, electricians, therapists, consultants) who are drowning in admin tasks. They know they should automate but don't have the time or technical know-how to set it up.

## Why They Pay
Time is money. Every hour spent on invoicing or chasing late payments is an hour not spent doing the work that pays them. They want their business to run smoother without hiring a virtual assistant or learning new tools.

## Simplest Version
Automate appointment reminders and follow-up emails for a single small business. Test with Pro Drain AK. If it works for Roy's plumbing business, it can work for others.

## Blocker
Pro Drain AK is the test case — if we can't demonstrate value there, we have no proof. Roy has limited time due to TurboTax season. We need a weekend or evening session to actually set up the automation and prove it works.

## First Step
Audit Roy's current workflow at Pro Drain AK. What does he do manually that could be automated? Scheduling confirmations? Invoice follow-ups? Text reminders? Pick the one biggest pain point and solve it first.

## Revenue Model
Monthly retainer: $99/month for basic automation (up to 3 workflows), $199/month for full automation setup (up to 10 workflows). First month free if we can use them as a case study.

## Competitive Edge
We're not selling software — we're selling a working automation system. We design it, implement it, and maintain it. Most consultants sell the tool; we sell the outcome. Pro Drain AK is our lab. We know OpenClaw's cron and automation capabilities — we can build this without external infrastructure we don't have.
