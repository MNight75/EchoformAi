# Plan 11: Cron Pipeline Design

## What We Sell
We design custom automation pipelines for businesses — sequences of cron jobs, agents, and workflows that run on a schedule and handle recurring tasks automatically.

## Who Pays
Small businesses and solo entrepreneurs who have repetitive workflows they want automated but don't have the technical background to design cron pipelines or coordinate multiple agents.

## Why They Pay
They have a workflow that works manually — "every Monday I send this report," "every time a client pays I do these three things" — and they want it to run itself. They need someone who understands both workflow design and OpenClaw's cron/agent capabilities.

## Simplest Version
$200 for a designed pipeline. Client describes their workflow in plain language. We design the cron schedule, agent roles, and data flow on paper. If they want us to implement it, that's a separate build fee.

## Blocker
Implementation liability. If we design a pipeline that breaks, deletes data, or spams clients, we're responsible. We need clear scope limits — we design, we don't always build. Or we build with heavy disclaimers.

## First Step
Document 3 example pipelines as samples: (1) weekly report generation, (2) invoice follow-up sequence, (3) daily digest for a solo business owner. Use Pro Drain AK's workflow as one of the examples. These become our portfolio.

## Revenue Model
Design-only: $200-500 depending on complexity. Design + build: $400-800. Monthly maintenance: $50/month for pipeline monitoring and adjustments. Design-only is safer — we don't inherit ongoing operational risk.

## Competitive Edge
We understand OpenClaw's cron system, agent memory, and workflow design intimately. We're not selling software — we're selling expertise. Most automation consultants sell generic Zapier setups; we design custom agent-driven pipelines specific to OpenClaw's capabilities. We already have the tools — no additional infrastructure needed.
