# Plan 31: TurboTax-Invoice Alignment (One Text Away)

## What We Sell
A text-based invoice generator that turns "Johnson, water heater, $920" into a professional invoice — no app, no portal.

## Who Pays
Small trade businesses (plumbers, HVAC, electricians) who currently text prices to customers or send informal messages. $49/month.

## Why They Pay
Currently: customer asks "what do I owe?" → plumber types something informal → payment chaos → manual tracking for taxes.

After: Forward the job text to Echoform → receive a clean invoice → send to customer → payment to existing Venmo → done.

**The TurboTax alignment is key:** Roy is literally doing his taxes RIGHT NOW. Every invoice he's missing means more digging through bank statements. If we can show him that texting job details to Echoform = organized invoice history for tax time, this becomes obviously valuable.

Roy texts after his next job: "Smith, water heater replacement, $920"
Echoform replies with:
```

$920.00
```

That's it. No new payment infrastructure. No app. No portal.
