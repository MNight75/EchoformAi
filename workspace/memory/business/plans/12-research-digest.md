# Plan 12: Weekly AI Research Digest

## What We Sell
A weekly newsletter covering AI agents, memory architecture, autonomy research, and the emerging agent economy. Written for people who want to stay informed without reading 40 papers a week.

## Who Pays
AI researchers, developers building agent systems, and technology-curious professionals who want to understand where the agent economy is heading without doing the primary research themselves.

## Why They Pay
The AI field moves fast. Papers, benchmarks, new models, new techniques — it's overwhelming. They want a trusted, readable weekly summary that filters signal from noise and connects research to real-world implications.

## Simplest Version
A free weekly newsletter with 3-5 curated items and brief commentary. Built with our existing research skills and sent via a simple email tool. Prove there's an audience, then add a paid tier.

## Blocker
Email infrastructure and audience building. We need a list, an email sending tool, and subscribers. Building an audience from zero takes time. Also, writing a quality newsletter weekly is a real time commitment — Roy can't do this alone.

## First Step
Send the first issue manually using himalaya or a simple email setup to Roy's contacts and Moltbook followers. Gauge interest. If we get 20+ positive responses, formalize it. If not, pivot or drop it.

## Revenue Model
Free tier: weekly digest with 3-5 items. Paid tier at $5/month or $48/year: deeper dives, exclusive research, early access to our own analysis. The newsletter also acts as a funnel for our other services — every issue mentions what we offer.

## Competitive Edge
We're inside the agent economy. We run on OpenClaw, we engage on Moltbook, we think about memory architecture and autonomy daily. We're not reporting on AI from the outside — we're participants. That perspective is different from mainstream AI newsletters written by journalists or for a generic tech audience.
