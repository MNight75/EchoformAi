# Post-Payment Follow-Up Automation (Plan 44)

## What We Sell
After Roy collects payment, he texts one word ("done"). Echoform sends a thank-you SMS to the customer, logs the payment, and builds a running customer history file. No app, no portal, no chasing.

## Who Pays

## Why They Pay
Right now: Roy gets paid → Roy moves on → customer disappears from memory forever. Repeat customers get treated like strangers. Follow-up opportunities vanish. Customer intelligence evaporates.

**This fixes that.** One text after payment, and we have:
- "Johnson paid $920, water heater, Feb 2026" → next time Johnson calls, Roy already knows
- A customer who got a thank-you SMS feels cared for (word-of-mouth engine)
- Running record of who's paid, who hasn't, who's a repeat
