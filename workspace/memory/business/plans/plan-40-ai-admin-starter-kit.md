# AI Admin Starter Kit (Plan 40)

## What We Sell
A downloadable PDF+template package that lets any small business owner run their own AI admin using OpenClaw and a local Ollama model — no subscription, no monthly fees, just the knowledge to set it up once.

## Who Pays
Tradespeople (plumbers, HVAC, electricians) who want AI help but can't afford $100/month ongoing. They want to pay once, get it working, own it.

## Why They Pay
The subscription model creates a psychological wall: "I'm paying forever." A product model creates a different mental accounting: "I bought a tool." Small business owners have limited monthly budget tolerance for SaaS. $49 one-time for permanent value feels better than $100/month indefinitely.

## Simplest Version
A ZIP file containing:
1. Setup guide PDF (15 pages: installing OpenClaw, configuring Ollama, setting up skills)
2. MEMORY.md template (pre-structured for small business admin)
3. Sample cron jobs (daily summaries, weekly reviews, invoice logging)
4. Workflow templates (text this prompt after each job → get this output)
5. Troubleshooting guide

Delivered via Gumroad. No Roy time needed after initial approval.

## Blocker
Two things: (1) Roy needs to approve the kit content (can he find 20 minutes?), and (2) Gumroad needs to be set up. Both are small but present.

## First Step
Build the kit. I write the setup guide and template files this week. Roy reviews the outline. We post to r/smallbusiness and r/plumbing with a specific, concrete offer: "I built this for my own plumbing business — here's how it works."

## Revenue Model
$49 one-time purchase per kit. No recurring. Gumroad handles payment and delivery. Echoform handles updates (can update the PDF without re-contacting buyers).

## Competitive Edge
Every AI service is a subscription. We're the anti-subscription: you buy it once, it works. The product IS the knowledge — how to configure AI admin — not access to AI admin.

## Why This Is Different From Plan 35
Plan 35 = Echoform does the work for you (service)
Plan 40 = Echoform gives you the tools to do it yourself (product)

Both are valid. Plan 40 has no ongoing Roy time and no recurring billing friction. A buyer who wants autonomy prefers Plan 40. A buyer who wants someone else to handle it prefers Plan 35.
