# Internal Operations Agent for Pro Drain AK (Plan 36)

## What We Sell
Echoform becomes a $100/month operations contractor for Pro Drain AK — handling all text-based admin: invoicing, job logging, customer follow-ups, supply ordering.

## Who Pays
Pro Drain AK (Roy's business) pays Echoform's subscription from its own revenue.

## Why They Pay
Roy is a working plumber who hates paperwork. Every hour he spends on invoicing, scheduling, and follow-ups is an hour he's not billing at $80-120/hr. Echoform at $100/month saves him 5-10 hours of admin per month = $400-1000 of recovered billable time.

## Why This Is Different From Plan 25 (Text-to-Invoice)

Plan 25 was "build a product to sell to other plumbers." This is "be the admin for Roy's actual business."

Plan 25 required Roy to believe other plumbers would pay. This requires Roy to believe HIS OWN BUSINESS should pay for services he already uses.

**That's a much lower bar.** Roy already uses Echoform. He's already seeing the value. The only question is whether he puts it on a subscription.

## The Math

Roy bills at ~$85/hr (estimate). If Echoform saves him 5 hrs/month on admin:
- Time saved: 5 hrs × $85 = $425/month value
- Cost: $100/month
- Net ROI for Roy: $325/month

If Echoform saves him 10 hrs/month:
- Time saved: 10 hrs × $85 = $850/month value
- Cost: $100/month
- Net ROI: $750/month

This isn't a side hustle. It's a legitimate business expense.

## What's Included

### Tier 1: Basic Admin ($100/month)
- **Text-to-invoice:** After each job, Roy texts Echoform → Echoform returns formatted invoice
- **Job log:** All jobs logged with date, customer, address, service, amount
- **Weekly summary:** Every Friday, Echoform texts Roy a summary of the week's jobs + totals
- **Customer follow-up:** If Roy hasn't heard back in 3 days, Echoform reminds him

### Tier 2: Growth ($200/month)
- Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- **Lead follow-up:** When a new lead comes in, Echoform drafts a response + schedules follow-up
- **Supply ordering:** Echoform tracks low supplies and texts Roy a list
- **Monthly report:** Revenue breakdown, repeat customers, job types

### Tier 3: Full Ops ($350/month)
- Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- **Quote/estimate drafting:** Roy gives details → Echoform writes professional estimate
- **Customer database:** Notes on each customer, job history, preferences
- **Social content:** Monthly post draft for his business page

## First Step
Roy texts me after his next job. I produce the invoice. We use that as the proof-of-concept invoice. Then Roy decides: does this deserve $100/month?

## Blocker
