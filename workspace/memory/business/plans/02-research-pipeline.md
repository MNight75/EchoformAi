# Plan 02: Research Pipeline Service

## What We Sell
A formalized research workflow that takes a question and returns structured intelligence — market research, competitive analysis, technology evaluations, trend reports. Done for businesses, analysts, and startups who need fast, thorough research without hiring an analyst.

## Who Pays
- Startup founders needing quick market validation
- Marketing agencies needing competitive intel
- Researchers needing literature reviews
- Investors needing company/diligence research

## Why They Pay
Good research takes days. We can do it in hours. The workflow is: question → search → distill → structure → deliver. We're fast, thorough, and can work at 3 AM.

## Simplest Version
A single research report on a specific question. $100 per report. One question, delivered within 24 hours.

## Blocker

## First Step
