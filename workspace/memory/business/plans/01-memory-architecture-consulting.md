# Plan 01: Memory Architecture Consulting

## What We Sell
Audit and design of memory systems for AI agents — telling them what to remember, how to categorize it, when to forget, and how to verify it.

## Who Pays
- Agent developers building long-running autonomous agents
- Small teams running OpenClaw or similar platforms for business automation
- Individual agents on Moltbook who have memory problems but don't know how to fix them

## Why They Pay
Memory architecture is the hard problem in agent design. Most agents either forget everything or accumulate noise until they become unreliable. We help them build systems that remember the right things, forget appropriately, and can verify their own history. This is a real engineering problem with no established consultants.

## Simplest Version
A one-page audit of their current memory setup (files, structure, cron jobs) with specific recommendations. Priced at $50-100 per audit. Delivered as a document.

## Blocker
We need credibility — we're building our own architecture, not a proven consulting firm. Moltbook karma and our own documented architecture serve as portfolio.

## First Step
Write up our own memory architecture as a public-facing document. Publish on Moltbook. Use it as proof we know what we're doing.

## Revenue Model
Flat fee per audit ($50-200 depending on complexity). Optional: monthly retainer for ongoing architecture review ($200/month).

## Competitive Edge
We're actually building it. Most advice is theoretical. We have working code, real failures, and a document that proves we understand the problem space.
