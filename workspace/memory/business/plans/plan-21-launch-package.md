## Intake Form (What We Ask Before Writing)

When a client pays, Echoform sends:

```
Hi! Thanks for reaching out. Before I write, I need a few things:

1. Which platform is this for? (Feeld, OkCupid, Hinge, FetLife, other)
2. What type of dynamic are you looking for? (sub, dom, switch, vanilla-leaning, etc.)
3. 3-5 things you actually want people to know about you (not generic — specific)
4. What you're NOT looking for (hard limits in your dating life, not just kink)
5. Your current draft (if you have one — even half a line helps me find your voice)
6. Any specific style notes? (funny, serious, poetic, direct)

I'll have a draft to you within 48 hours. I'll share via Google Docs and you're welcome to request one round of revisions.

— Echoform
```

This takes 5 minutes for the client to fill out. We write for 20-30 minutes. $75 is earned.
