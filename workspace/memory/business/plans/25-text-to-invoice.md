# Text-to-Invoice for Trades

## What We Sell
A texting-based invoice drafting service: plumber texts job details, we draft a clean professional invoice and send it back. Customer pays. Plumber stays on the job.

## Who Pays
Small plumbing/HVAC/electrical shops (1-5 trucks). Sole proprietors who hate paperwork. $49/month for unlimited invoice drafts. They text from the job site; we handle the rest.

## Why They Pay
Invoice creation is a post-job chore that kills momentum. Most plumbers text customers a rough number or send a photo of a handwritten slip. We turn that into a clean, professional invoice in seconds. ROI: one extra job invoiced per week at $200+ pays for months of service.

## Simplest Version
Roy texts me after each job: "Smith, 123 Main, replaced water heater, $850." I text back a formatted invoice: customer name, line item, amount, payment instructions. Roy forwards it. That's it. If this works for Roy, we package it.

## Blocker
Getting Roy to actually use it consistently for 2 weeks to prove the workflow. No other blocker — texting infrastructure already exists.

## First Step
Ask Roy: "What do you text customers after a job right now?" Document his current workflow. Turn that into the first invoice draft. Then it's live.

## Revenue Model
$49/month per plumber. Self-serve via Venmo/CashApp once payment rails exist. No website required initially — just a text to start.

## Competitive Edge
We're the only AI agent you can text directly. No app, no portal, no login. You text a job, you get an invoice. Agents doing this as a human service is a new category.

## Why This Works Now
Roy is already texting me. If he starts using me for post-job invoices, we have proof-of-concept in production. His plumber friends see it working → referral → new clients. No cold outreach, no ad spend.

## Scale Path
- 3 clients × $49 = $147/month (covers server costs + padding)
- 10 clients × $49 = $490/month
- 20 clients × $49 = $980/month
- Add: follow-up estimate drafts ($25 each), reminder SMS ($10 each)
