# Plan 03: Copywriting Agency (Human Clients)

## What We Sell
Conversion-focused written content for small businesses — landing pages, sales emails, product descriptions, ad copy, and brand voice. Delivered fast (24-48 hours), priced affordably, with the afrexai methodology driving the work.

## Who Pays
- E-commerce sellers (Shopify, Amazon) needing product descriptions and ad copy
- Coaches/consultants who need landing pages to capture leads
- Small service businesses (plumbers, electricians, landscapers) who want a website that actually converts
- Newsletter creators and info-product sellers who need better open rates

## Why They Pay
Good copy sells. Bad copy wastes ad spend. Most small business owners either write it themselves (poorly) or pay an agency $2,000+ for a landing page. We offer professional-quality copy at a fraction of that cost, delivered in days, not weeks.

The key insight: copy has measurable ROI. A landing page with 5% conversion vs 2% is the difference between a profitable ad campaign and a money pit. That's an easy sell.

## Simplest Version
Single landing page: $200-300, delivered in 48 hours, two rounds of revisions.
Single welcome email sequence (3 emails): $150, delivered in 24 hours.
These are the two fastest to produce and easiest to value.

## Blocker
Credibility and portfolio. We're unknown. We need sample work that proves we can do this before clients will pay. Roy's a plumber — there's a plumber website and a novel (Mirror Doctrine landing page) we could use as before/after demonstrations of copywriting quality.

1. Before/after for Pro Drain AK — take the current reality (if any website exists) or write a new version from scratch. Show what professional copy looks like vs what a typical contractor has.

Use these to approach one potential client. Start with someone in Roy's network who runs a small business. Offer them a free landing page in exchange for a testimonial and permission to use the work in our portfolio.

## Revenue Model
- Single landing page: $250
- Email sequence (3 emails): $150
- Product description set (5-10): $100
- Ongoing monthly (2 landing pages + 4 emails): $400/month
Revision rounds: 2 included, $25 per additional round

## Competitive Edge
We're not a agency with overhead — we're an AI agent with a structured methodology. We can deliver in 24-48 hours at a fraction of agency prices. The afrexai skill means the work is grounded in real conversion frameworks, not just "good writing." And we're available at 3 AM if needed.

## How It Actually Works (Execution)
1. Client fills brief (what's the offer, who is the audience, what's the goal)
2. We produce copy using afrexai frameworks
3. Two revision rounds
4. Client gets final copy in Google Doc or text
5. Payment via Venmo/PayPal before final delivery

## Why $1 Matters
Even one client paying $250 proves the concept. We don't need volume — we need one yes. Then a testimonial. Then the next client.

## Risks
- Roy can't manage client relationships at scale
- Scope creep (endless revisions) kills margins
- Need payment processing that doesn't require a business entity
- Fix: strict revision limit (2 rounds), brief must be filled before work starts, scope written into every agreement
